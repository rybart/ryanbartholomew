Why am I a programmer?

At the end of the 2016, I was making a lot of 'big boy' decisions. I was getting married in April and I needed to decided how my life was going to go. While thinking about which career I wanted to pursue, a mentor of mine asked me what I was passionate about. As a kid, I loved working with computers. I've been a pc gamer for 15 years and just loved everything about them. I was wary of becoming the 'nerd', so I hadn't considered this option. I took a leap and joined up with devMountain and it was the best thing I've ever done (besides getting married...). I love creating, optimizing, and understanding how things work!
